How to choose between lace, skin, and mono.

The three base families solve three different problems. Lace breathes, skin disappears, mono endures. Pick the problem you most need solved, and the base picks itself.

Most first-time buyers start with the wrong question — “which one looks best?” They all look right when they're specced right. The better question is about your routine: how much time you'll give it each week, and what you refuse to give up.

“Pick the problem you need solved. The base picks itself.”

Lace is the choice when hairline realism and airflow rank first — expect a slightly longer care routine. Skin at 0.03–0.08 mm wins on invisibility and daily convenience. Mono is the workhorse: highest density support, longest lifespan.

Still torn? That's what the consultation is for — bring your routine, we'll bring the decision tree.
